Advanced queue management system

This project is my greatest achievement so far, it's basically an advanced queue management system which was proposed by the Tunisian company "WINCOM" during my end of studies intership there.
If you have a service providing business such a bank, post office or any other type of business where your customers have to wait in a queue in order to benefit from one of your service then this application is all you need to manage your queues and customer flow.
I've given the administrator the ability to organize his services and counters in any way he likes and the ability to centralize all of his business branches data if he has more than one geographical site in his business, he also has the history of every single client that signed up in a queue and which counter agent served every client which is useful for handling customer complaints.
Finally the administrator posesses a real-time statistcs dashboard that helps him monitor the queues precisely in each branch of his business, know the peak hours, the most in-demand service and the abandonment rate of customers.

Now we go to the customer part of the app, since this is web application the customer can now receive a virtual ticket using his smart-phone and is no longer required to be physically present to wait his turn, he can just sign up in a queue using his phone and wait his turn as he goes on with his day.
Before you get your ticket though you can know exactly how many customers are in queue before you and how much time you will wait approximately.
I've also developed a tickets kiosk module of course where the customer can still get a paper ticket in the traditional way.

Moving on the counter agent part of the application, each agent is now more informed of what's going on in his queue, he can know exactly how many customers he has, how many "absent" customers there is and an approximate time to serve all customers in the queue.
He controls the flow by notifying the system that he's either "available" or "unavailable" and he can mark a customer as "absent" if he is called to the counter but doesn't show up.
Finally the last part of the application is the counters display, it's that big screen you find in every queue system where each ticket number is displayed infront of the counter number that called that ticket, and of course the numbers are updated in real-time whenever an agent makes a call, voice call also included.
The administrator can also use the counters display screen to display a written message in order to inform the present customers of something.

I've been as brief as possible in this description there are much more smaller details in the app, but I've covered the most important parts already.
This project helped me greatly to master React.js and Node.js and hone my problem solving skills, it was a really great experience to develop a web application with this scale.
This application is developed to work on digital terminals and any other device with a web browser installed.
No code source or live version available for this project since it's a private one but you can see screenshots of it by clicking on the link.
Technologies: React.js, Node.js, PostgreSQL
